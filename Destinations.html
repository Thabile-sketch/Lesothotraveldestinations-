Destinations

Explore breathtaking places of Lesotho

Lesotho is a prime location for hiking,camping,cave exploration

horseback riding and even snow sports during winter months.

Katse Dam

The second largest double dam in Africa constructed in 1990s which is

710 meters long and 185 metershigh and also impounds a deep reservoir

that runs more than 30kmalong the Malibamatso river when full.

Maletsunyane Fall

One of the highiest dropping waterfalls in the Southern Hemisphere

which plummets 192 meters into a spectecular gorge creating clouds

visible from afar.A nearby lodge(Semonkong lodge)offers you a

chance to explore the magnificent scenery on the waterfall.

Tsehlanyane National Park

It is located in the Southern Maluti Mountains,150km northeast

of Maseru which is considered to be Lesotho's most accessible

park and it is known for its rugged montane vistas,stunning

waterfalls and an excellent network of hiking and horseback trails.

Sehlabathebe National Park

Lesotho's first national park established in 1970 and it protects 65

square kilometers of grassland set below the uKhahlamba Drakensburg

escarpent,running along the border with South Africa.
